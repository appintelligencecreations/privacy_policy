Privacy Policy - Alien News

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Alien News ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension for accessing UFO and alien-related news.

Information Collection

Alien News collects minimal information to provide its service:

  • News reading preferences and settings
  • Temporary cache of news articles for offline reading
  • Basic usage statistics to improve service quality

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API and is not transmitted to any external servers except for RSS feed requests.

Data Usage

The collected information is used solely for:

  • Displaying relevant UFO and alien-related news
  • Maintaining your reading preferences
  • Improving the news aggregation service
  • Providing a better user experience

Data Protection

Your privacy is important to us. We protect your data by:

  • Storing data locally on your device
  • Not collecting any personal information
  • Not sharing any data with third parties
  • Using secure connections for RSS feed requests

User Control

You have full control over your data:

  • You can clear the news cache at any time
  • You can customize your news preferences
  • Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's documentation and this privacy policy page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through:

  • Chrome Web Store support channel
  • GitHub issue tracker
  • Extension feedback form
