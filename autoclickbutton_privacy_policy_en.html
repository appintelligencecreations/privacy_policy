Privacy Policy - Auto Click Button

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Auto Click Button ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection

Auto Click Button collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Website URLs you configure for automatic clicking
  • Button selectors you specify for clicking
  • Scheduled click times and frequencies
  • Language preference settings

All data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Data Usage

The collected information is used solely for:

  • Executing automated button clicks according to your schedule
  • Maintaining your click plan configurations
  • Providing the extension's core functionality

Data Protection

Your data is stored locally on your device and is protected by Chrome's built-in security measures. We do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Use your data for any purpose other than providing the extension's functionality

User Control

You have full control over your data:

  • You can view all stored click plans in the extension interface
  • You can modify or delete any click plans at any time
  • Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's documentation and this privacy policy page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support channel.
