Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 16th, 2024

1. Information Collection and Use

The Exam Schedule Assistant extension does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information to external servers. We only store the following information locally on your device:

  • Exam names and dates you enter
  • Your language preference settings
  • Your selected country/region settings

This information is used solely to provide the core functionality of the extension, which is to help you manage your exam schedules.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not transmit or store any data on external servers.

3. Data Access and Control

You can view, modify, or delete your stored exam information at any time through the extension interface. Language and country/region preferences can be easily changed in the extension settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. All your information is strictly kept on your device.

5. Security

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, please note that no electronic storage method is 100% secure.

6. Changes

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page and update the "Last updated" date at the top.
